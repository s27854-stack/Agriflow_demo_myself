# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Home gardeners and small-scale farmers who monitor soil moisture and automate watering via ESP32 sensors. They use the dashboard to check real-time sensor data, configure watering thresholds, and track moisture history. Context: checking from phone/tablet while tending plants, often outdoors with variable lighting.

## Product Purpose

Agriflow makes smart watering accessible to non-technical users. The dashboard visualizes soil moisture data, controls watering duration/thresholds, and provides clear status feedback (valve open/closed, countdown timers). Success means users can keep plants healthy without manual monitoring.

## Brand Personality

Approachable & Clean. Friendly, trustworthy, nature-connected. Not intimidating. Think "helpful gardening assistant" not "industrial control panel."

## Anti-references

- Complex enterprise SCADA/PLC dashboards with dense data grids
- Generic SaaS aesthetics (cream backgrounds, purple gradients, glassmorphism everywhere)
- Overly technical interfaces with jargon-heavy labels
- Data overload without clear visual hierarchy

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over complexity**: Show only what matters at each glance
2. **Nature-connected**: Visual language should feel organic, not industrial
3. **Mobile-first**: Primary use case is phone/tablet while gardening
4. **Progressive disclosure**: Details available on demand, not forced upfront
5. **Accessible by default**: Readable in sunlight, works for all abilities

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AAA compliance (full)
- High contrast for outdoor/sunlight viewing
- Large touch targets (min 44px)
- Screen reader support with ARIA labels
- Reduced motion support
- Readable at all viewport sizes
